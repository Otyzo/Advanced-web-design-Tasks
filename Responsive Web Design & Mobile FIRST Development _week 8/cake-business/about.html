Our story

From a home kitchen to a Nairobi studio

Crumb & Co. started in 2016 as a one-woman cake stall and grew into a full training studio and supplies counter for Nairobi's home bakers.

How we got here

Founder Wanjiru Karanja baked her first wedding cake for a neighbour in 2016, using a borrowed oven and a single piping tip. Word spread fast, and within two years she was teaching weekend classes out of her own kitchen to keep up with requests.

Where we are now

Today, Crumb & Co. runs a dedicated training studio in Westlands and stocks everything a home baker needs — fondant, tins, turntables, and colour — so students can walk straight from class into their own kitchen, fully equipped.
